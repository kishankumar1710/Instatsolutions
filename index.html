About Instat Solutions

Instat Solutions is a statistical analysis consultancy founded and led by Dr. B K Rakshith, specializing in biostatistics, oral epidemiology, and data-driven health research. We serve dental researchers, academic institutions, and public health organizations across India — bridging the gap between complex clinical data and meaningful, evidence-based conclusions.
